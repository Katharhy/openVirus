Abstract

2.1

ACE2 is located on the X chromosome and is one of the genes escaping X inactivation (Tukiainen et al.,Â 2017). Respiratory droplets that spread every time while breathing, coughing, or sneezing are the main route of transmission of SARSâ€�CoVâ€�2.
